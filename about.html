About FAZZ

We believe technology can illuminate possibilities and improve students' lives.

FAZZ (Future Advancements in Zero Barrier Zone) is a student-led nonprofit made up of developers who create practical technology solutions for students.

Who we are

FAZZ is a community of student developers who build technology with purpose.

Our mission

To create technology solutions that improve students' lives by building accessible tools that support learning, organization, and everyday challenges students face.

Our vision

A future where every student has access to thoughtful technology that supports how they learn, plan, and grow.

What we value

We value collaboration, learning, accessibility, and building software that helps real students.

Our founders

Meet the students who started FAZZ.

Our approach

We focus on projects that are useful, thoughtful, and built for real student impact.

FAZZ is not a personal portfolio or startup. It is a nonprofit community where students collaborate, learn, and build technology that improves student life.
