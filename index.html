Our mission

Our mission is to create technology solutions that improve the lives of our community — students and elderly alike.

Who we are

FAZZ is a community of student developers who believe technology can create meaningful change when it is built with care.

What we build

We create practical apps and tools that help students plan and learn, and help elderly community members stay connected and confident with technology.

Why it matters

Every project is shaped by real student needs, thoughtful collaboration, and a commitment to positive impact.

Highlighted projects

Our work centers on useful technology, clear impact, and student collaboration.

Community outreach

We host in-person events for the communities we build for: students and elderly community members.

From coding workshops to tech help drop-ins, our outreach events bring FAZZ's apps and developers directly to the people who use them.
